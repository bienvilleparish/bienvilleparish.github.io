TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 2. TEACHERS AND EMPLOYEES

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

 

17:419.1     Minimum salary schedules for school support personnel

  The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall formulate, develop, and recommend to the legislature, statewide minimum salary schedules for school support personnel. The board shall submit the recommended minimum salary schedules for such personnel to the House and Senate Committees on Education not later than March 1, 1993, for consideration by the legislature at the 1993 Regular Session. The recommended statewide minimum salary schedules shall include but need not be limited to instructional aides and paraprofessionals, school bus drivers, food service workers, school nurses, clerical, custodial, and maintenance personnel, and any other employees of a parish or city school board or unclassified noninstructional employees of the state schools for the deaf, blind, spastic, and cerebral palsied and Special School District No. 1 who are not required to hold a teacher's certificate as a condition of employment, and shall be based on implementation beginning with the 1993-1994 school year, but only to the extent that state funds are provided for such implementation.

Added by Acts 1992, No. 510, Sec. 1, eff. June 22, 1992.
