TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 2. TEACHERS AND EMPLOYEES

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

 

17:422.4      Adjustments in salaries of vocational-technical institute instructors

  Notwithstanding any other provision of law, at any time the legislature appropriates funds to provide for a cost-of-living salary adjustment, or any increase in the minimum salary schedule, or any other generally applicable salary increase for public elementary and secondary school teachers, such salary adjustment or increase shall also apply to instructors of all state supported vocational and technical institutes.

Added by Acts 1991, No. 1018, Sec. 1, eff. July 25, 1991.
