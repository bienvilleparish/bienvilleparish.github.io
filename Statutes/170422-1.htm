TITLE 17. EDUCATION

CHAPTER 2. TEACHERS AND EMPLOYEES

PART I. GENERAL PROVISIONS

 

17:422.1      Adjustments in salaries of employees not covered by minimum schedules

  Simultaneously with the gradual implementation of the minimum salary schedule for teachers provided in R.S. 17:421, as amended by Act No. 28 of the Extraordinary Session of 1964, and as further amended by Section 1 of Act 397 of 1968, not less than a ten per cent per year increase in salaries for each of the next four years, beginning with the school year 1968-69, shall be granted to all nonprofessional school employees including janitorial, custodial, maintenance, school lunch and all other nonprofessional school employees of the parish and city school boards of this state who are not covered by the minimum salary schedule for teachers or the minimum salary and/or bus operation schedule applicable to school bus operators provided however that the benefits granted each employee under this section for the above mentioned employees shall not exceed four hundred dollars per school year and further provided that increases normally granted these employees shall not be reduced or eliminated because of the increases granted under this section. At no time subsequent hereto shall the salary of any employee covered hereunder be less than the salary received in 1967-68.

Added by Acts 1958, No. 330, Sec. 1. Amended by Acts 1964, Ex.Sess., No. 28, Sec. 1; Acts 1968, No. 397, Sec. 2.
