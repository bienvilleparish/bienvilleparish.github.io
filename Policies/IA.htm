INSTRUCTIONAL PHILOSOPHY

 

 

In its pursuit of excellence in preparing a child for life, the Bienville Parish School System believes that every student has the inherent right to the best education that can be provided.  The Bienville Parish School System is dedicated to establishing and following educationally sound objectives that enhance quality instructional programs.

 

PURPOSES

 

The major purposes of the Bienville Parish School System are as follow:

 

  1. To make the schools an essential key to better life for all students.

  2. To implement an academic program which provides each student the opportunity to realize individual potentialities.

  3. To maintain standards for student achievement.

  4. To develop school effectiveness through continuing evaluation which leads to improvement in instruction and the learning environment.

  5. To promote an attitude which leads to the continued enhancement of professional competencies among all school personnel.
