STUDENT COMPLAINTS AND GRIEVANCES

 

 

Students have both the right and the responsibility to express school related concerns and grievances to the administration.

 

Grievances of students should be handled expeditiously and under proper procedures.  Any student grievance shall follow the same procedures as outlined for Bienville Parish School Board employees.
