DRUG FREE WORKPLACE

 

 

The Bienville Parish School Board supports and endorses a drug-free workplace policy. Recognizing the need to protect individual employee rights, nothing herein this policy shall be construed to waive any rights granted by state or federal law.  However, the public safety of children has been entrusted to its employees and the first responsibility of this school system is to maintain that trust.

 

EDUCATION

 

The Bienville Parish School Board shall offer to its employees a drug-free awareness program which will provide employees information and education about the following issues:

 

  1. The dangers of drug abuse in the workplace.

  2. The Bienville Parish School Board policy regarding the maintaining of a drug-free workplace.

  3. An available referral source for assessment and treatment.

  4. Disciplinary action that may be taken against employees for any violation of the Drug Free Workplace policy.

 

A biennial review by the Bienville Parish School Board shall be conducted to evaluate the policy's effectiveness and consistent enforcement.
