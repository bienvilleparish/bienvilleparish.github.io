ADMINISTRATIVE PERSONNEL

SICK LEAVE

 

 

Sick and emergency leave shall be granted with pay to all 12-month full-time employees on a longevity basis as follows:

 

A.

 First three years - 12 days each year

B.

 Next six years - 15 days each year

C.

 Beginning with 10th year -  18 days each year

 

    

Emergency or personal - 

  two (2) of the total allowable days

 

All other provisions governing sick and emergency leave shall be as outlined under policy GBRIB, Sick Leave.

 

 

Ref:    La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §§17:81, 17:1201, 17:1202
