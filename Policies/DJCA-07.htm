PAY DAY SCHEDULES

 

 

Salary payments for Bienville Parish School Board employees shall be made on a 12-month basis.  Effective November 2007, salary distributions will be on the 25th of each month, with the exception of January, which will be on the 20th.  If the payroll date is on the weekend or holiday, disbursements will be made on the last working day prior to the date.

 

Revised:  October, 2007
