HEALTH INSURANCE REGULATIONS

 

 

ELIGIBILITY GUIDELINES FOR RETIREES' INSURANCE PREMIUMS

 

Retiree Share

 

All employees hired on or after July 1, 1995 must work a minimum of five (5) consecutive years in Bienville Parish immediately preceding his/her retirement in order  to be eligible for the Bienville Parish School Board to pay a portion of the retiree's share of the insurance premium.

 

The total retiree’s health insurance premium paid is a combination of the state's share and the retiree's share.  For employees hired on or after July 1, 1995, Bienville Parish will pay a percentage of the retiree's share (not to exceed the Magnolia Open Access rate) of the insurance premiums based on the number of years worked in the Bienville Parish School System prior to actual retirement as follows:

 

Years Worked
in Bienville Parish

Percentage of
Retiree's Share Paid by Board

20+

100%

15 - 19+

75%

10 - 14+

50%

5  -  9+

25%

 

The Bienville Parish School Board will pay the retiree’s share for a retiree’s single rate without Medicare or the retiree’s single rate with Medicare, not to exceed the Magnolia Open Access rate of the ‘Office of Group Benefits Official Schedule of Premium Rates.’  The retiree’s rate identified on the Office of Group Benefits Official Schedule of Rates at 19%, 38% and 56% Participation will not apply.

 

State Share

 

Effective January 1, 2002, the Bienville Parish School Board will pay the state's share of retirees' insurance premiums according to the state subsidy schedule outlined in La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §42:851, as follows:

 

Less than 10 years

19%

10 to 15 years

38%

15 to 20 years

56%

More than 20 years

75%
